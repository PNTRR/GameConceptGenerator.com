About the Project

Game Concept Generator

A tool for overcoming creative block

The Game Concept Generator is an online tool designed to quickly overcome creative block and generate unconventional ideas for game projects. It requires no registration, is free to use, and operates on the principle of intelligent randomness.


Its goal is to become your digital co-author at the very earliest and most challenging stage of development: the search for a unique concept.

How does it work? Simplicity for complex tasks


The principle of "random collision" in three clicks:


  1. You click the "Generate" button.
  2. The algorithm combines random elements from an extensive database of key game design components: setting, core mechanics, genre foundation, player goal, and visual style.
  3. You receive a ready-made, provocative statement — a raw, unpolished idea. For example: "A post-cyberpunk survival simulator where you manage a traveling theater, and victory is achieved through environmental performances."

Your task is not to accept it as an instruction, but to use it as a springboard for your own thinking.

The generator does not write plots or create design documents. It creates conditions for insight by intentionally colliding concepts that your pattern-prone brain would not normally connect.

You are not searching for an idea — you are solving a creative puzzle that it throws at you.


This makes it not a source of answers, but a trainer for creative thinking, helping you break out of the cycle of habitual solutions.

Who is this tool for?


The tool has been tested and proven useful for a wide range of professionals and enthusiasts:


  • For indie developers and small studios: To quickly find a "hook" for a new project or an unexpected twist for an existing one, moving beyond competitors.
  • For game designers and narrative designers in large companies: To conduct warm-ups and brainstorming sessions, training mental flexibility away from corporate briefs.
  • For students and beginners: To overcome the fear of a blank slate, get a first concrete starting point for a study project, and learn to think outside the box from the very start of their career.
  • For everyone who loves games and fantasizing: To simply enjoy playing "What if?" and generate amusing or brilliant concepts.

Key Features and Benefits


What you get when you start working with the generator:


  • Instant Result: One button — one ready-to-contemplate concept. No waiting, no complex settings.
  • Unlimited Source of Ideas: The database is constantly updated, and the combinatorics algorithm ensures a practically infinite number of unique results.
  • Focus on Essence: The statements are stripped of filler; every line is a potential gameplay or narrative hook.
  • Stimulus for Teamwork: A generated concept is the perfect starting point for a team discussion. "How could we implement this?" is the best question for a brainstorm.

The Game Concept Generator is your personal brainstorming moderator, always at hand. It won't replace deep analytical work, but it will radically speed up and invigorate the very first — and most creative — phase of any project.
