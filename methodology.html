Methodology

Combating CCDI Through Structured Randomness

Our approach addresses Creative Crisis in Developed Industry (CCDI) by providing structured inspiration rather than complete solutions. The generator acts as a creative catalyst, not a solution provider.

Why This Works Against CCDI

The generator's power lies in what it doesn't provide. By offering fundamental elements instead of complete concepts, it:

  • Forces creative problem-solving
  • Prevents design paralysis from too many complete options
  • Encourages personal interpretation and expertise application
  • Creates space for unique combinations that reflect your vision
  • Reduces the pressure of creating "something completely new"

The Creative Process

Each generation should be treated as a creative prompt, not a final concept. The real work begins when you start asking "how" and "why" these elements belong together.
